    Filing an online complaint by foreign workers about the violation of their rights to the Commissioner for Foreign Workers at Work

    This service allows foreign workers to file a complaint with the Commissioner for Foreign Workers regarding violations of their rights during employment or at its termination, and to receive a response concerning their rights.

    Share:
    Online
    Submit a complaint

    Who can file a complaint?

    • Employees who believe their rights have been violated or who have not received all of their entitled rights.
    • Anyone who wishes to file a complaint against employers, in the event of a suspected violation of labor laws.
    The service is available and accessible in several languages: Hebrew, English and Arabic.

    What documents should be attached to the complaint?

    • First and last name of the complaining employees.
    • Contact telephone number.
    • Employer details (name, address, name of direct manager and phone number).
    • A brief description of the complaint.
    • Documents related to employment.
    • For an anonymous complaint (without a name), attaching identifying information is not required.

    How to submit a complaint

    1. You must enter the complaint submission form for foreign workers and select the "Submit a complaint" option.
    2. Fill out the details in the online form (which can be completed without identification) and submit it.
    3. Those who submit the complaint will receive a complaint number, which can be printed or saved on their computer.
    • To fill out the complaint, no government identification is required.
    • You can submit either an anonymous (unnamed) complaint or one with your personal details, according to your choice

    Follow-up on complaint process

    • Email – send to the Commissioner at: Foreign.Rights@labor.gov.il
    • Phone (WhatsApp):  050-6290758
    • Phone: call 074-6290758
    1. Once the complaint is received in the system, it will be reviewed.
    2. After reviewing the complaint, the Ministry representatives will contact the employees, if needed, to complete the necessary documents for addressing the complaint.
    3. The complaint will be handled and the employees will receive updates on its status.
