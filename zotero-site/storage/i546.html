    The digital Apostille

    The Ministry of Justice has launched today a groundbreaking, digital end-to-end online service for approving notarized Israeli documents that are required for use abroad, known as an apostille

    Type:
    News
    Publish Date:
    09.02.2022

    The Ministry of Justice has launched today a groundbreaking, digital end-to-end online service for approving notarized Israeli documents that are required for use abroad, known as an apostille.

    An apostille is a certificate issued by a competent authority of a state, on public certificates, confirming that the certificate is official, original and issued by the competent authority. 

    The new service was favored in the government's digital acceleration program due to its importance in reducing the bureaucratic burden and improving the service to many citizens and partners in it.

    You no longer need to travel to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Jerusalem or physically go to the courts to obtain a verification certificate for a public certificate or an Israeli notarized certificate - It is now digital, quick, and easy and is delivered directly to your inbox!

    All you need to do is push a button, it’s really that simple!
